Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic treat loved by everyone. This homemade recipe is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth with its perfect balance of soft and chewy texture and rich chocolate chips. Give it a try and enjoy the deliciousness!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare Baking Sheets:
    • Line two baking sheets with parchment paper or use non-stick baking sheets.
  3. Sift Dry Ingredients:
    • In a small bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour and baking soda. Set this aside.
  4. Cream Butter and Sugars:
    • In a large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter, granulated sugar, light-brown sugar, and salt until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  5. Add Eggs and Vanilla:
    • Add the eggs, one at a time, and beat well after each addition. Stir in the pure vanilla extract.
  6. Incorporate Dry Ingredients:
    • Gradually add the sifted dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  7. Add Chocolate Chips:
    • Stir in the semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed in the cookie dough.
  8. Drop Dough onto Baking Sheets:
    • Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving some space between each cookie for spreading.
  9. Bake:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes or until the edges of the cookies are lightly golden. The center should still be slightly soft. Be careful not to overbake.
  10. Cool:
    • Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, then transfer them to wire racks to cool completely.
  11. Serve:
    • Once the cookies are completely cool, serve and enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies!

Indulge in these delightful homemade chocolate chip cookies and savor the sweet moments they bring.
