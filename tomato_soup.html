Homemade Tomato Soup

Warm and comforting, homemade tomato soup is a classic favorite. This recipe is simple to make and perfect for a cozy meal. It's the perfect way to enjoy the rich and tangy flavors of fresh tomatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté the Aromatics:
    • In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic, and sauté until they become soft and fragrant.
  2. Add Tomatoes and Sugar:
    • Add the chopped tomatoes and sugar to the pot. Cook for about 10 minutes, or until the tomatoes have softened and released their juices.
  3. Blend the Mixture:
    • Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the tomato mixture until smooth.
  4. Return to the Pot:
    • Return the blended mixture to the pot. Stir in the vegetable broth and bring the soup to a simmer.
  5. Add Cream and Season:
    • Stir in the heavy cream, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve:
    • Ladle the homemade tomato soup into bowls, garnish with fresh basil leaves (if desired), and serve while hot.

Enjoy your delicious homemade tomato soup, a perfect comfort food for any occasion.
